About KFC

KFC is an American food resturant, founded in the 1900's. KFC is one of the most popular restaurant chains by revenue, serving over 1 million customers daily . Although is best known for its chicken, hamburgers, cheeseburgers, sweets and french fries, , breakfast items, soft drinks, milkshakes, wraps, and desserts. In response to changing consumer tastes and a negative backlashbecause of the unhealthiness of their food, the company has added to its menu salads, fish, smoothies, and fruit.
